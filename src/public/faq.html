USSD Wallet Questions

Simply dial *384*2005# on any mobile phone (even feature phones). Follow the prompts to set up your 4-digit PIN, and your Hedera wallet will be created automatically. No app download or internet connection required!

Yes! Hflow uses military-grade security:

  • Your private keys are encrypted with your PIN (AES-256)
  • AI fraud detection monitors all transactions
  • Non-custodial - you control your HBAR
  • All transactions recorded on Hedera's immutable ledger

For security reasons, PINs cannot be recovered. However, you can:

  1. Visit an authorized Hflow agent with your registered phone and ID
  2. Complete biometric verification
  3. Set up a new wallet (your old HBAR balance will be transferred)

This process takes 3-5 business days for security checks.

Fees & Limits

  • • Sending HBAR: 0.1% (min $0.01) + Hedera network fee ($0.0001)
  • • Cashing out to M-Pesa: 1.5% (max $5)
  • • Cross-border (Nigeria → Kenya): 2% (vs. 8% with traditional services)

Fees are dynamically calculated. Dial *384*2005# and select "Estimate Fees" for real-time quotes.

To comply with Kenyan regulations, these limits apply:

Unverified Users

  • Max send: $50/day
  • Max receive: $200/day
  • Wallet balance: $500 max

Verified (KYC)

  • Max send: $5,000/day
  • Max receive: Unlimited
  • Wallet balance: $50,000 max

Visit any Hflow agent with your ID to increase limits.

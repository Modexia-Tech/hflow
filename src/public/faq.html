Technical Questions

We provide multiple recovery options:

  1. Secret Recovery Phrase: During registration, you'll receive a 12-word phrase that can restore your wallet on any device
  2. Phone Number Verification: You can recover using your registered phone number and ID verification
  3. Agent Assistance: Visit any HPesa agent with your ID to recover your account

We recommend writing down your recovery phrase and keeping it in a safe place.

Transaction times vary by type:

  • HBAR transfers: 3-5 seconds (Hedera network speed)
  • Cross-chain transfers: 2-5 minutes (depends on blockchain congestion)
  • M-Pesa cash-out: Instant to 5 minutes

All transactions are confirmed via SMS once completed.

Our transaction limits are:

  • Minimum: 10 HBAR (≈$0.10) or equivalent
  • Maximum (unverified users): $100 equivalent per day
  • Maximum (verified users): $10,000 equivalent per day

Higher limits available for enterprise customers.

We use a combination of:

  • Real-time market data from multiple exchanges
  • Hedera's native price oracle
  • Local currency feeds from central banks

Exchange rates are locked in when you initiate a transaction and confirmed before completion. You'll always see the exact amount you're sending/receiving before confirming.
